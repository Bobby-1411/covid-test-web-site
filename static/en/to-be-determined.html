Your sample has been analyzed but the result is not yet conclusive

In a small number of our tests (less than 1 in 200) the result is inconclusive. This may be because the test yielded a false positive result, or the sample is very weakly positive and we are not sure.

To verify this, we re-analyze these samples by RT-qPCR and using a kit in the viral diagnostics unit. This takes a maximum of 24 hours.

You need to check back here in about 24 hours to get the diagnostic result.

It is possible that you are in the early stages of an infection, or in a very late stage of the infection, and therefore you have (still) very low values. Rarely an inconclusive Result can be caused by a contamination in your sample that can influence our test and therefore we get an unclear result. A diagnostic test using RT-qPCR helps us to clarify the situation.
